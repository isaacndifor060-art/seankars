Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 3 to 6 months in advance for weddings and large corporate events to ensure availability and ample planning time. For smaller parties, 4 to 6 weeks is usually sufficient.
Do you provide styling items or just design?
We are a full-service styling company. We provide all the props, centerpieces, backdrops, lighting, and floral arrangements needed to bring the design to life.
Do you travel for events?
Yes! While we are primarily based locally, we are available for destination weddings and events. Travel and logistics fees will be included in your custom quote.
